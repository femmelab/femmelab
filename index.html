Why FemmeLab?

We dream and work towards a world where people of all genders have an equal share of opportunities to tell their own stories and write their own destinies. More than different representations in media, we seek to transform media systems to be more equitable, critical, and caring.

Feminist Media Lab (FemmeLab) is a community-centered organization focused on: (1) designing participatory media processes so that women and gender-diverse individuals can voice out their own agenda and claim space for their desires; and (2) facilitating consciousness-raising using creative media to cultivate feminist imaginations and critical thinking.

More about us

Impact Areas

Our work is geared towards creating and driving impact on the areas of media production, empowerment of women and gender-diverse people, feminist organizing, and feminist knowledge production.

Gender-Responsive Media

We help mainstream gender to organizations and media practitioners through custom-designed toolkits and modules

Feminist Organizing

We support feminist organizations and gender advocates in leveraging media for their advocacy work through media co-productions and knowledge sharing

Creative Self-Expression

We empower diverse groups of feminists, girls, women, transgender, and gender diverse individuals through creative media workshops

Feminist Media Innovation

We develop and implement feminist media practices and processes that would help create a more equitable, critical, and caring media system
