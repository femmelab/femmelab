How can we build a feminist media community?

Kommunity Kitaan

We sharpen and share our feminist critical lens through regular community meet-ups that feature learning sessions on emerging and persistent gender issues in media and society-at-large. We invite partners, allies, and advocates in this participatory gathering where everyone could give their take and recommendations on the issues and challenges raised.

Given feminism’s transformative ambitions, more work is necessary in order to develop theories, concepts, and language that are meaningful to policy makers and to citizens and link feminist media scholars and activists in their efforts to “empower” people to understand, consider, assess and critique the media content they produce and/or consume in ways that make for greater social justice and minimize unfair discriminations.

– Linda Steiner
