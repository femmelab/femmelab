Our Story

Feminist Media Lab (FemmeLab) started in late 2019 with a spark of inspiration: how do we use and transform media in order to realize feminism’s alternative vision of our current society, one where people of all genders have a voice in matters that shape their imaginations, realities, and futures? Backed by a close-knit community of feminists and allies, this inspiration turned into small collaborative projects. Today, FemmeLab serves as a media advocacy hub that seeks to arouse and amplify feminist voices, narratives, and visions through innovations in media production and community-building.

Our Values

Transformation and Disruption

We believe in transformation that disrupts the status quo towards greater social justice

Reflexivity and Care

We embrace critical reflection and providing each other with care as paths to empowerment

Diversity and Creativity

We celebrate diversity as a manifestation of creativity and a source of power in collective action
