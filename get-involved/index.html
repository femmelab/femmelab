Everyone has a place in FemmeLab

Work with Us

Feminist Media approaches can apply wherever you desire to promote gender equality, diversity, and the empowerment of women and LGBTQI+ people.

Collaborations are possible across diverse sectors and demographics. You might be an organization focusing on development, health, environment, education, social innovation, youth, women’s rights, LGBTQI+ rights, persons with disabilities, design, technology, media, advertising, or governance, among others.

Internship Program

"[Gusto ko] yung pagiging community-centered [ng FemmeLab], kahit pa maliit lang yung community namin na yun, nandun yung elements na open lahat, open ang communication, nakakapag-coordinate ng maayos, you’re able to work together more smoothly. Walang napag-iiwanan."
-Nadine, 2020 Intern
"My favourite concept is the incorporation of feminism and gender equality in the media that you produce, [and how] it’s already a way of resistance in itself…being a feminist media practitioner is already a way of resisting and changing things…ang pagiging babae mo in itself [is a] form of resistance."
-Maan, 2020 Intern
"I was part of a writers’ workshop and I was trying to write a manuscript for a graphic novel. Na-stuck ako … Until naging intern ako sa FemmeLab, at mas naisip ko pano iincorporate ang concepts of feminism and gender rights in media, it helped me write my story and improve my characters."
-Dana, 2020 Intern

Interns are a critical part of our operations.

If you want to be part of something meaningful, take on essential roles, and learn to apply feminism in media and creative work, then apply to us as an intern.
